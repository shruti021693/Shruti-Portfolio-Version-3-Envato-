Blend Wine Subscription

Overview

Some urban young professionals feel overwhelmed while selecting wine, translating their preferences, and seeking notable suggestions from professional wine drinkers.

  • Duration :

    6 weeks

  • Team :

    Solo

  • Role :

    UX Research, Brand Development, Wireframing, visual design, UI Interface

  • Skill :

    Illustrator, Photoshop, Procreate, XD, Webflow

Problem

How might we provide a wine club experience which would reduce wine anxiety while educating wine drinkers?

Solution

I uncovered wine selecting obstacles from urban young professional wine drinkers and used social networking to help them connect to others on advice about wine selections. The website and app empower and educate users to learn about wine by hanging out with club members and cultivating a growing local wine community.

Research

Hard seltzer is a resilient item within the entire alcoholic beverage industry due to being gluten-free and low in alcohol content. Also, a major reason behind the increasing adoption of hard seltzer is the reduced content of calories and carbohydrates in comparison to beers and other mixed drinks.
