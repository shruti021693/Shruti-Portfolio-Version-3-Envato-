Today's Task

Background

Many people struggle with staying organized and remembering their everyday duties. They may forget important tasks or delay their completion. This can lead to increased stress and decreased productivity.

  • Duration :

    4 weeks

  • Team :

    Individual Project

  • Role :

    UX Design, Design Thinking, and Research, Interaction, Mockup designs

  • Skill :

    Figma, Adobe Creative Suite

  • Client :

    SleeplessYogi

Problem

How can we enhance users’ productivity by enabling them to efficiently complete their daily tasks? How can we help users in completing their tasks and monitor their productivity?

Solution

A new tab on the Chrome browser focused on randomly selected tasks that provide a simple and user-friendly platform to create and manage daily tasks. Today's task can help increase productivity by tracking progress and encouraging them to complete the work.

Discover

Research

To start my research, I decided to conduct a competitive analysis of similar applications/websites available in the market to identify opportunities and threats.

Free for basic functions but require a subscription to get efficient value out of it. Clean and engaging UI but the improvement required over ‘difficulty understanding the app’. The app involves lots of features that end up distracting users. Also, pop-up ads are annoying for users.

The app allows users to create lists, and share, and also allows them to set reminders, add tags and sync will all devices for a premium subscription. Opportunity in redesigning the UI with clean, simple, and intuitive designs.

Findings
In summary, users face difficulty in selecting the ideal app or website because of the abundance of options available in the market. most of them require a subscription fee to access essential features, which is not affordable for 80% of the users. Additionally, they have a plethora of features such as reminders, tags, sharing, and syncing, which can be overwhelming and confusing for users.

Define

Persona

According to research, 82% of people struggle to manage their time to complete their work. Usually, they fail to manage their task by not having a plan, or a clear goal, and procrastination by considering minor tasks. So I crafted my user personas as a reference point to better emphasize the uses and not to lose focus on their needs and wants.

User Journey Map
Selection of Chrome Extension

-Chrome is one of the most widely used browsers, for kids to baby boomers. And it helps to target a larger audience compared to different platforms.
-Also, it can quickly and easily access the browser to manage users' daily task lists.
-Every new tab of the Chrome browser prompts/encourages users to complete their daily tasks, offering them a more efficient method of managing their tasks.

User Interface Design

After a few sets of iterations, I successfully incorporated the feedback and finalized the designs for User Interface. I created Light and dark theme versions.

Deliver

I delivered the project and assisted the team in successfully launching it as a Google Chrome extension.

Conclusion

Overall, I thoroughly enjoyed working on this challenging project. The ideation process was successful in providing a simplified version of the solution, after collaboration with the developer. With this new tool, users can track their progress and stay motivated to complete their tasks.
